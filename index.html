Description

Exporecerca Jove is an international research fair held annually in Barcelona. Its objectives are to promote research among young people, to promote the exchange of experiences and knowledge, and to promote participation in other fairs and congresses of national and international scope.

Format

The fair is divided in two phases, the fair and the final. During the fair, around 90 different projects are presented by the students. During the final, only celebrated for category C participants, some selected students from the fair present their project in a 10 minute talk.

Participants

The fair is celebrated for people from 12 to 19 years old. They are divided in different categories, A for students from 1st and 2nd of ESO, B for students from 3rd and 4th of ESO, C for students from high school and E for international students.

Awards

Unlike other fairs, Exporecerca Jove gives the awarded students the opportunity to attend other national and international fairs such as the Regeneron ISEF.

History

This fair started in 1999 and now has celebrated a total of 25 editions, having had over 4800 participants with more than 2400 projects over this period.
